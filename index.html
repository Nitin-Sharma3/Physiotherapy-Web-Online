Flexyra Specialized Treatments

At Flexyra, we provide a comprehensive range of physiotherapy services tailored to your unique journey toward optimal health and movement.

Cupping Therapy

Experience the benefits of cupping therapy to ease muscle tightness, improve mobility, and promote natural healing for lasting comfort.

Myofascial Release (MFR)

A hands-on technique that relieves muscle tightness, reduces pain, and restores flexibility by releasing restrictions in the connective tissues.

TKR / THR Rehabilitation

Personalized rehabilitation after Total Knee or Hip Replacement to restore strength, mobility, balance, and confidence in everyday movement.

Back Pain Treatment

Effective physiotherapy to relieve back pain, improve posture, strengthen supporting muscles, and prevent future discomfort.

Cervical Pain

Specialized treatment for neck pain and stiffness to improve mobility, reduce discomfort, and restore healthy movement.

Sciatica Treatment

Targeted therapy to relieve sciatic nerve pain, reduce numbness, and improve mobility through evidence-based physiotherapy techniques.

Pre-Operative Rehabilitation

Prepare your body before surgery by improving strength, flexibility, and mobility to support a smoother recovery.

Post-Operative Rehabilitation

Guided rehabilitation after surgery to reduce pain, restore movement, rebuild strength, and help you return to daily activities safely.

Sports Injury Rehabilitation

Recover from sports injuries with customized treatment focused on pain relief, performance recovery, and injury prevention.

Gym Injury Treatment

Expert care for muscle strains, ligament injuries, and workout-related pain to help you recover safely and return stronger.
